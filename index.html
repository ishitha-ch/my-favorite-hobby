The Books I Read


I wanted to give a few reccomendations and tell you what I read.

Harry Potter

The above are the original 7 Harry Potter books. Then came Cursed Child which is an epilouge and it's about Harry's son(and it's a screenplay which can performed).
Harry Potter finds out he's a wizard! And he's off to Hogwarts. He has to defeat Voldemort, can he do it? These books would be good for older kids especially because there is so much information that younger kids may not remember.


Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Percy Jackson and the Olympians is a series of 5 books.The stories are based off of Greek Mythology. Percy finds out that he is the son of one of the Gods. He goes to Camp Half-Blood and trains to become a hero. These books would be good for middle-school-aged kids because younger kids may not understand them. You also need to know a little bit about Greek Myths or you will have to search them up in order to completely understand them.


Magic Tree House

The Magic Tree House books are some of the best for a young kid. I only put four pictures because this series is huge! The author is still writing them and two came out in 2020! This series is about Jack and Annie who use the tree house go to different places and different times in history. They meet famous people too! These books are for young readers. My brother is in first grade and he loves them!
